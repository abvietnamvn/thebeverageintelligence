Lager Category

Lager beer is the main export category of A&B Vietnam and represents the largest share of production output. This line is optimized for high consistency and global consumer compatibility.

Production applies bottom fermentation using Saccharomyces pastorianus with controlled temperatures from low temperature fermentation range to ensure clean profile.

Ale Style Beer
